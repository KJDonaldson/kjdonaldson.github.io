## Research Experience

**PhD Student — Neuroscience Institute, Georgia State University**  
2018–2025  

- Investigated cellular and molecular mechanisms of experience-dependent cold nociceptive plasticity in *Drosophila*
- Combined super-resolution microscopy, electrophysiology, calcium imaging, and optogenetic circuit manipulation
- Contributed to identification of neural mechanisms underlying cold acclimation
- Developed a high-throughput open-source optical stimulation platform (“Optobox”) for optogenetic experiments

---

**Research Technical Specialist — Emory Eye Center Bioanalysis Core**  
2017–2018  

- Trained research staff and students in confocal microscopy and IMARIS 3D analysis
- Developed automated segmentation software for retinal imaging datasets
- Assisted investigators with gene network and bioinformatics analyses
- Co-managed the imaging core confocal microscope facility

---

**Research Specialist — Nickerson Laboratory, Emory University School of Medicine**  
2015–2017, 2019–2025  

- Generated transgenic mouse lines enabling longitudinal in vivo imaging of retinal pigment epithelium
- Investigated RPE dysmorphology in mouse models of retinal degeneration
- Conducted immunohistochemistry and confocal imaging analyses

---

**Graduate Researcher — University of Maryland, College Park**  
2006–2015  

- Studied orbitofrontal cortex modulation of auditory perception using in vivo electrophysiology
- Mapped frontal–sensory cortical connectivity using neural tracing methods
- Investigated neural network activity during auditory task performance using c-fos immunohistochemistry
- Contributed to MEG studies of auditory perception in collaboration with linguistics researchers
